Business Credit Availability Program (BCAP)

This advice was last updated on 04/08/20 at 13:38 PST.

The Government of Canada has established a program to provide $40 billion of additional support through the Business Development Bank of Canada (BDC) and Export Development Canada (EDC).

What it is

This program includes loan guarantees for new operating credit and cash flow term loans of up to $6.25 million, and co-lending term loans for operational cash flow requirements, to eligble businesses.

Who is eligible

You are eligible if you are a small or medium-sized business.

How to access

Program will roll out in mid-April. Interested businesses should work with their current financial institutions.
