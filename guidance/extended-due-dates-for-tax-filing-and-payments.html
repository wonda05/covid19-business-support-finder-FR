Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) – COVID-19 Relief Provisions - Extended Due Dates for Tax Filing and Payments​

This advice was last updated on 04/07/20 at 22:20 PST.

The CRA has provided some relief to taxpayers and filers of other information returns by extending due dates to provide more time to file and pay as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.

What it is

The CRA has extended due dates for tax filing and payments to allow organizations more time to file tax returns and to defer payments.

Who is eligible

All Taxpayers and Filers of Other Tax Information Returns

  • Individuals
  • Self-employed (and their spouse or common law partner)
  • Trusts
  • Partnerships
  • Corporations
  • Charities
  • Payers of Part XIII Non-Resident Tax
  • Filers of Other Tax Information Returns

How to access

No application needed.
