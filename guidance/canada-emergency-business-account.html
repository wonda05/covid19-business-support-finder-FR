Canada Emergency Business Account

This advice was last updated on 04/07/20 at 00:00 PST.

A new federal program will be rolled out in mid-April that helps to cover operating costs for small businesses and non-profits experiencing temporary revenue reductions.

What it is

Interest-free loans of up to $40,000 to help cover operating costs during a period where revenues have been temporarily reduced.

Who is eligible

You are an eligible if you:

  • Are a:
    • Small business
    • Not for profit organization
  • Have paid between $50,000 to $1 million in total payroll in 2019

How to access

Program will roll out in mid-April. Interested businesses should work with their current financial institutions.
